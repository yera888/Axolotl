About this site

This is a simple, educational site about axolotls. It aims to look like a small scientific page: calm colors, a clear serif font, and clean layouts. Every page uses the same top navigation.

Why axolotls?

Axolotls are salamanders that keep their juvenile traits into adulthood. They can regrow limbs and parts of organs, which makes them interesting to biologists. They prefer cool, clean water and gentle flow. Good care keeps them healthy and active.
