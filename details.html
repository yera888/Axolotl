Axolotl — details

This page gives more background about axolotls in general. All cards on the home page can link here.

Quick facts

  • Species: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Known for limb and organ regeneration
  • Native to the Xochimilco canal system, Mexico City
  • Cool water: about 16–18°C (60–64°F)

Care basics

  • Clean water, gentle flow; avoid strong currents
  • Fine sand or bare bottom; avoid gravel
  • Protein-based diet: worms, pellets, bloodworms
  • Provide hides and low light areas
