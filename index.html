Axolotl Morphs

This page shows several common axolotl morphs. Use the search box to filter the list by name or keywords.

Leucistic

Pale body with dark eyes and often pink gills.

Wild-type

Speckled, dark/olive colors similar to natural look.

Melanoid

Deep, even coloration with a matte finish.

Golden Albino

Warm golden tone; lacks dark pigment.

Albino White

White body and light eyes; sensitive to strong light.

GFP

Glows under UV due to green fluorescent protein.

Firefly

Rare look; often shows GFP in the tail region.
